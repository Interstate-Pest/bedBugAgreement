Nature and Scope of Agreement: Bed Bug Inspection and Treatment Plan.
The agreement contained within has been designed for the management of bed bugs. In low and moderate level infestations with proper client cooperation elimination is possible, however, in high level infestations, there is no guarantee that bed bugs will be eliminated or prevented from continuing to infest the treated areas. Bed bugs are an introduced pest and the re-occurrence of bed bugs within the facility can occur at any given time
